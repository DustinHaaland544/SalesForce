The Salesforce Certified Administrator Exam consists of:

- 60 scored multiple-choice questions

- 5 unscored pretest questions (these are for research and do not count toward your score)


Breakdown:

- Total questions: 65

- Questions that count: 60

- Questions that don’t count (pretest): 5 (randomly mixed in, you won’t know which)


Time Limit:

- 105 minutes (1 hour and 45 minutes)


Passing Score:

- 65%

	- So you need at least 39 correct answers out of 60 scored questions.
