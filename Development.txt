To become a strong Salesforce developer, here are some things you can do:

Take online courses or earn a degree in a related field such as computer science or information technology.

Familiarize yourself with the Salesforce platform and its various features and capabilities. You can do this through the Salesforce Trailhead learning platform 
or through self-study.

Get certified in Salesforce development. Salesforce offers a number of certification exams that test your knowledge of the platform and its development tools.

Gain practical experience by working on real-world projects and building custom applications on the Salesforce platform.

Stay up to date with the latest trends and best practices in Salesforce development by reading industry blogs and participating in online communities.

Consider joining a Salesforce user group or attending Salesforce-related events to network with other professionals and learn from experts in the field.
